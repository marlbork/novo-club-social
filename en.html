Private Investment & Project Structuring

Novo Club Social

Private capital for projects with genuine execution capacity, structured return potential and economic, social or environmental impact.

Nature of the group

Proprietary capital, selective analysis and direct participation.

We operate as a private investment structure. We are not a financing platform, broker, financial intermediary or financial advisory entity.

Each opportunity is reviewed individually before considering any investment, co-investment or tailored financing structure.

Operational framework

Access to analysis or participation is neither open nor automatic.

Each opportunity is subject to a prior review before any investment consideration is made. The objective is to identify projects with genuine development capacity, execution potential and structured return opportunities.

  • Technical, operational and financial assessment.
  • Defined or scalable management structure.
  • Alignment between capital requirements, risk and expected return.
  • Progressive validation before activating structuring resources.
Investment criteria

Projects with structure, viability and purpose.

01 Preliminary analysis

Real feasibility

Technical, operational and financial assessment before moving towards any participation structure.

02 Controlled execution

Milestone-based execution

Investment may be deployed through agreed phases, aligning capital, project progress and oversight.

03 Strategic impact

Sustainable value

Priority is given to projects capable of generating employment, territorial development and measurable positive impact.

Areas of interest

Sectors and projects

Social impact

Initiatives that promote employment, training, local productive capacity and new opportunities in distressed areas.

Environment and energy

Projects linked to sustainability, efficiency, energy transition and environmental improvement with a solid structure.

Real estate and hospitality

Assets or developments with repositioning potential, disciplined operation and territorial value creation.

Infrastructure

Operations with economic or social utility, clear planning and a technically viable execution framework.

Procedure

A private, orderly and progressive process.

  1. Phase I Preliminary review

    LOI submission, NDA, project documentation, initial assessment and feasibility validation.

  2. Phase II Structuring and execution

    Indicative terms, MOU, investment structure, milestone-based deployment and project monitoring.

Oversight and control

Investment is supported by monitoring, milestones and project-specific annexes.

Each transaction may include a dedicated annex defining structure, amounts, phases, timetable, economic terms, control framework and exit mechanisms.

Final implementation remains subject to technical, regulatory, operational and institutional validations. Preliminary acceptance of a project does not constitute a guarantee of final funding or transaction closing.

Information notice

The information on this website is provided for descriptive purposes only. It does not constitute a public investment offering, solicitation of savings or deposits, financial advice, brokerage, intermediation, or any binding commitment to provide financing or execute transactions.
